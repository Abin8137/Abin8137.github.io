My creative process and tools i used

Market Research.

In the beginning phase, the goal is to learn as much as possible about your market and your individual goal.

Roadmap.

A road map of the UX process is created. This roadmap includes milestones that will take you through.

Wireframing.

A wireframe is a simple, skeletal outline of a design. It represents only the structure and layout of the service being built.

Prototyping.

The prototype is where the design starts coming to life. An interactive, working design is completed in a design tool that you can try for yourself.

User testing.

Before going live, the design has to be tested by users. This is to identify which sections or areas of the website that can be improved.

Design completion.

The final touches have been made, and the finished website is up and running.
